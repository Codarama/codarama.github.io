Diet

Put your project on a diet!

Diet is a Java-based tool that analyses your source code for it's dependencies and makes sure only directly referenced files are included in your distribution package.

There are three distinct ways to run Diet :

  • Directly work with the engine - as you would use any other Java framework
  • Use our Maven Diet Mojo - add Maven goal as part of your existing build
  • Use our Gradle Plugin - similarly to the Maven mojo, but for Gradle builds

If your deliverable includes your dependencies they would be replaced with a minimized JAR. If not you will be given a path to the minimized JAR to use as you please.

Codarama HaxSync

Better Android Contact Pictures

Isn't it a shame that all your contacts maintain an up-to-date, recent photo of themselves in thw social networks, but when they call you on your phone you mostly get a gray anonymous face?

HaxSync was initially developed by MAthias Roth, but after he was invited to join the Facebook team the project became abandoned. The idea behind it is to use the available Facebook APIs and extract recent, high quality pictures of the contacts you have in your phone.

Future releases have the ambition to include other social networks so that you could alwasy have a choice of the best photos
